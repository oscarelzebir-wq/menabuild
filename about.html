Our Story

Building Florida
the Right Way

Menabuild was founded with a single mission: to build homes the right way. Based in Princeton, FL, we've spent years earning the trust of homeowners across Miami-Dade and Broward counties by delivering projects that are built to code, built to last, and built around you.

We understand South Florida's unique challenges — hurricane codes, humidity, and the fast pace of the local market. That's why every Menabuild project starts with a detailed plan and ends with a home you're proud to live in.

From our first project to our 200th, our commitment hasn't changed: deliver exceptional craftsmanship, communicate openly, and treat every home like it's our own.
